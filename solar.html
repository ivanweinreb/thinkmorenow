Project Status

1. Project Kick-off & Asset Collection

The initial meeting with Paul McCormack was highly productive. We successfully defined the scope, selecting the primary house roof model and receiving the flagship product's rotating video. The foundational camera angles were established, starting with an isometric rotation and incorporating angles inspired by the official company website.

View Camera Angle Reference Visit SolarTileRoof.us

2. Creative & Technical Direction

As per Paul's direction, the project's primary objective is aesthetic appeal to drive sales, focusing on showcasing the product in the best possible light rather than technical minutiae.

To achieve this, Ivan Weinreb has proposed leveraging Unreal Engine's advanced lighting capabilities. This will allow for dynamic control over sunlight, shadows, and ambient conditions, synchronized with camera movements to create a visually stunning and motivational presentation.

3. Asset Processing & Delivery

We received the STEP file for the flagship solar tile. Subsequent files were successfully converted to the more accessible GLTF format (as binary .glb files), empowering our client with better control and review capabilities. Additionally, 6 sets of non-solar tile images and various supporting documents (DOC, JPG, PNG, PDF) were delivered as received from the Solar Tile Roof team and approved by Ivan's team for digital twin creation.

With all initial files received, processed, and approved, we can confidently conclude the pre-production phase. We are fully prepared to advance to the next stage: Realistic translation of product and environment layout.
