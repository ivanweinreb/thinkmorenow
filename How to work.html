How We'll Work Together

A simple, secure guide to our project on Freelancer.com using Milestone Payments.

1. Award Project

You accept my proposal and click "Hire Me". This officially starts our project contract.

2. Create Milestone

You fund the first agreed-upon task. The money is held securely by Freelancer.com, not paid to me yet.

3. Release Payment

I deliver the work. Once you're 100% satisfied, you release the funds. We then repeat for the next milestone!

Why This Protects You

Total Security

Your money is safe in escrow until you approve the work.

Complete Control

You only pay when you are 100% happy with the results.

Full Transparency

We agree on tasks and costs upfront for each phase.
