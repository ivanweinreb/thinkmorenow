Product Development & Launch Roadmap

Click the + buttons on each phase for more details!

Phase 1 - Completed

Concept Visualization

Bringing the concept to life with a detailed 3D model and renders of the SaniPaws system using Blender.

Day 15 of 15 (Started: July 10, 2025)

Key Tasks:

  • Block-out modeling of the dome, tunnel, and core components.
  • Basic modelling of the treadmill and internal mechanisms.
  • UV unwrapping, texturing, and material creation.
  • Generate 3-5 high-resolution photorealistic renders for review.
  • ✅ Draft the "Next Actions" document outlining the path forward.
Phase 2 - In Progress

Formal CAD & Engineering

Developing precise, manufacturable CAD models ready for prototyping and engineering analysis.

10% complete

Key Tasks:

  • Convert 3D model to parametric CAD format (e.g., STEP).
  • Optimize geometry for manufacturability (e.g., draft angles, wall thickness, tolerances).
  • Detailed mechanical design of the treadmill assembly and housing.
  • Material specification and tolerance analysis for all parts.
  • Create a complete Bill of Materials (BOM).
Phase 3

Prototyping & Evaluation

Creating physical prototypes to test functionality, ergonomics, and aesthetics for real-world refinement.

0% complete

Key Tasks:

  • Generate STL files for 3D printing of all custom parts.
  • Assemble the first full-scale physical prototype.
  • Conduct functional tests: treadmill movement, cat entry/exit flow.
  • Gather feedback on usability, cleaning, and cat acceptance.
  • Implement design changes in CAD based on evaluation results.
Phase 4

Manufacturing & Launch

Finalizing the design and delivering all files and recommendations for manufacturing partners.

0% complete

Key Tasks:

  • Finalize CAD models and create 2D manufacturing drawings.
  • Develop a Request for Quotation (RFQ) package for vendors.
  • Identify and provide recommendations for potential manufacturers.
  • Deliver all final design files, documentation, and BOM.
  • Testing first production units.
  • Generate merchandise designs.
  • Generate advertising media.
